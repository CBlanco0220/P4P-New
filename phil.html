About Philip Truong

Phil Truong is a full-time powerlifting coach and competitive lifter based in Orlando, FL. With an 815 kg total at 93 kg, a 519 DOTS score, and 106 IPF GL points, he brings elite-level experience both on the platform and behind the scenes as a coach.

He holds a degree in Sport Management with a minor in Business, combining academic insight with years of real-world application. With over 7 years of competitive experience and 5+ years coaching lifters from their first meet to the world stage, he’s helped athletes break personal records, earn podium finishes, and build lasting confidence through training.

Known for his communication and attention to detail, Coach Phil keeps lifters focused, informed, and progressing with purpose — all while creating an environment where athletes believe in their potential both on and off the platform.
